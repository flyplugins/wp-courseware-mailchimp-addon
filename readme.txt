== Description ==
= Subscribe students to your list upon enrollment =
Are you looking to subscribe your students to your email list? It's now possible with the MailChimp add-on for WP Courseware. Upon enrollment, you can subscribe your students to one of the following [MailChimp](https://flyplugins.com/mailchimp) elements:

* audience
* tags

Once you configure your MailChimp API Key and Secret you can begin configuring your courses.

== Installation ==

1. Go in to your WordPress Admin Panel.
2. Navigate to Plugins &rarr; Add New and click upload.
3. Browse for the wp-courseware-mailchimp.zip file and click upload.
4. Click Activate.
